Who Am I ?

After secondary school this almost completely disappeared because I decided to become a soldier. Around 2020, this interest returned and I first started looking for information online and soon started distance learning applied computer science.

1 year later I got the opportunity to be trained as a full-stack developer through Switchfully and I have been programming ever since. I love coding and constantly learning new things.
